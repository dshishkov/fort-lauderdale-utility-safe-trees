What this list includes

Only the city's 15-20 foot mature height small shade trees are shown here. Larger trees from the same PDF were intentionally removed. The goal is simple: give you a cleaner planting reference for below-line locations while keeping a short, practical warning about root behavior near foundations, plumbing, irrigation, septic components, and other buried services.

Foundation and utility legend

These risk labels simplify how root behavior usually shows up in a home landscape. They do not override site prep, setbacks, or utility locating.

Low risk Usually manageable root systems with lower odds of heaving pavement or pressing into nearby utilities when planted sensibly.
Moderate risk Still approved under power lines, but roots may spread wider, chase water, sucker, or fill tight beds more aggressively.
